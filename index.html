MY STORY

Everyone is a hero in their own story. My beginning starts as a middle school teacher. For a long time I always wanted to do something in my life that was meaningful, fulfilling, and highly creative. It was not long before I realized there was more to me than creating for a classroom of kids. I realized I wanted to live what I teach. I wanted to find my own purpose and fulfilling in my own skills. A friend of mine made the decision to change her life and as she told me about UX Design, I realized that I wanted more than anything to create solutions with my own hands. The rest is history.
